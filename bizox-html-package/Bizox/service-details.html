DICE — Digital Interactive Customer Engagement.

In today's rapidly evolving digital landscape, customer loyalty depends on more than a good product — it requires seamless, personalised engagement across every interaction. Loyalty programmes that fail to leverage the right technology quickly become irrelevant, unable to deliver the meaningful rewards experiences that modern customers and employees expect.

Our DICE (Digital Interactive Customer Engagement) platform is Pinnacle Rewards' proprietary full-stack loyalty technology solution. DICE powers everything from member-facing mobile apps and USSD solutions to sophisticated campaign engines, merchant management systems, e-commerce marketplaces, and advanced analytics — all in one integrated, scalable platform built for African market realities.

What is the DICE platform?

DICE is Pinnacle Rewards' proprietary loyalty technology platform that powers end-to-end programme management — combining two essential pillars of any successful loyalty programme:

Member-facing technology: The mobile app, USSD, and e-commerce marketplace that programme members interact with directly — designed to be accessible, intuitive, and rewarding for all income segments across Africa.

Operator-facing technology: The campaign engine, member and merchant management systems, analytics dashboard, and AI-powered intelligence tools that enable your team to design, manage, and optimise your loyalty programme at scale.

Why DICE matters

Investing in a loyalty programme without the right technology is like building a house on sand. The DICE platform gives your programme a solid, scalable foundation — one that enables you to launch quickly, engage meaningfully, and grow sustainably. It bridges the gap between global loyalty best practices and the specific needs of African markets, where mobile penetration, diverse income levels, and USSD accessibility are critical realities. DICE ensures every member, regardless of their device or data access, can meaningfully engage with your programme and feel the value of being rewarded.

What DICE delivers

The DICE platform gives your loyalty programme everything it needs to engage members, reward behaviours, manage merchants, and drive measurable business results — all in one place.

Mobile App & USSD: A branded member-facing app with full loyalty functionality, plus USSD access to ensure all members — regardless of smartphone access — can engage with the programme and redeem rewards.

E-Commerce Rewards Marketplace: A fully integrated marketplace where members can browse and redeem rewards across a curated network of merchants — from daily essentials like groceries and airtime to aspirational lifestyle rewards.

Campaign Engine: A powerful campaign management tool that enables targeted, personalised promotions and bonus point campaigns — driving specific member behaviours, increasing engagement, and boosting redemption rates across your entire member base.

Advanced Analytics & AI: Real-time data dashboards and AI-powered insights that continuously optimise your programme — tracking member behaviour, identifying engagement trends, and providing the intelligence needed to make smarter, faster decisions.
