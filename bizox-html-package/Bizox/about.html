Pinnacle Rewards (Pty) Ltd Established

Pinnacle Rewards was founded in Johannesburg, South Africa by Terry Moodley — an entrepreneur at heart, a banker by profession, and an accountant by training. The company was established with a clear purpose: to build Africa's most impactful loyalty rewards network by combining deep banking industry experience with a passion for rewarding the unrewarded.

Partnership Agreement with Loylogic — Zurich, Switzerland

Pinnacle Rewards completed a landmark partnership agreement with Loylogic, the global leader in loyalty and engagement solutions, headquartered in Zurich, Switzerland. This partnership enabled Pinnacle Rewards to combine its African market expertise and cultivated relationships with Loylogic's cutting-edge loyalty technology platform, including Pointshub and Pointspay.

Partnership Agreement with Qix — Venice, Italy

Building on its growing global network, Pinnacle Rewards completed a partnership agreement with Qix in Venice, Italy. This expanded the company's international reach and deepened its suite of loyalty technology solutions available to African clients, reinforcing Pinnacle's position as a globally connected, locally focused loyalty rewards partner.

Partnership Agreement with Loyalty One — Toronto, Canada

Pinnacle Rewards completed a third major global partnership agreement with Loyalty One in Toronto, Canada. With partnerships now spanning three continents — Europe, North America, and Africa — Pinnacle Rewards had firmly established itself as an internationally accomplished loyalty rewards partnership network with the global infrastructure to deliver world-class programmes.

African Bank Contract Awarded

A defining milestone in Pinnacle Rewards' history: the African Bank Ltd contract was awarded. Together with Loylogic, Pinnacle Rewards set out to design and build a first-of-its-kind loyalty programme for African Bank — one built on the principles of accessibility, inclusivity, and measurability, aimed at improving the financial wellbeing of all South Africans.

DICE Platform Development

Pinnacle Rewards developed and advanced its proprietary DICE (Digital Interactive Customer Engagement) technology platform — a full-stack loyalty technology solution encompassing mobile app, USSD, an e-commerce marketplace, a campaign engine, member and merchant management systems, and advanced analytics and AI. DICE became the technological backbone of Pinnacle's Rewards as a Service offering.

Audacious Rewards Programme Launch

Audacious Rewards was launched for African Bank customers — initially available to MyWORLD transactional account holders. The programme featured a merchant network covering daily needs (groceries, airtime, mobile data) as well as aspirational rewards, and incentivised positive financial behaviour such as credit score improvement. Within the first month, over 57,000 members joined and more than 50,000 credit reports were requested.

International Loyalty Award — Dubai 2024

In 2024, Pinnacle Rewards and African Bank's Audacious Rewards programme won the International Loyalty Award in Dubai — honouring the Best Short Term Loyalty Initiative. This global recognition cemented Pinnacle Rewards' position as a world-class loyalty solutions partner and validated its mission to deliver impactful, inclusive loyalty programmes for leading organisations across the African continent and beyond.
